About National Cadet Corps

History of NCC

The NCC was formed in 1948. It traces its roots to the University Corps created under the Indian Defence Act of 1917 to strengthen the youth and build national character.

Mission

To develop disciplined, responsible and committed citizens.

Vision

To empower youth to become potential leaders and responsible citizens.

Our College Unit

Our college NCC unit operates under the 4 Karnataka Battalion with a strength of 100 cadets (SD/SW).
